About

The Architecture
of Live Events

Icarus Protocol is an international event strategy and production management firm.

We work closely with each project from the early idea stage through to the event itself. We help shape the concept, plan the details, manage the budget, coordinate artists, work with suppliers, and stay involved on site to make sure everything runs as it should.

Each project is delivered together with local promoters and licensed production teams. This allows us to operate in full compliance with local regulations while maintaining a consistent level of quality across different markets.

Beyond our work as a production partner, we also independently initiate, invest in, and operate events under our own brand — taking full ownership from concept through execution.

At the core, we bring clarity, structure, and accountability to complex productions.

We are not the headline.
We are what makes it work.

Services

What We Do

From first concept to final curtain. We manage every layer of the event lifecycle.

01
Strategic Consulting
We assess the opportunity, define the right positioning, and build an event strategy that makes sense for the market and the audience.
02
Concept Development
We shape the idea into something real. That means the event narrative, the programming logic, and how the whole experience comes together.
03
Artist Booking & Coordination
We handle the full artist side. Finding the right names, negotiating deals, managing contracts and riders, and staying close on the day.
04
Budgeting & Financial Planning
We build the budget from the ground up and track it all the way through. No surprises, no gaps.
05
Supplier Management
We source, contract, and coordinate every vendor on the ground. Stage, sound, lighting, security, catering, and everything in between.
06
Production Oversight
We are on site. From setup to the final hour, we manage the floor so the event runs exactly as planned.
